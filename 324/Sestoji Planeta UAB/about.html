About Us

The 6th Planet, in the orbit of the Sun, is Saturn, and as most of the locals would describe it – here is the land of “Šeštoji Planeta”. We are basically a modern business solutions provider, creating an opportunity to live a better life for every other person out there.

A diverse team, based on professionals in the field and shouldered by common, ambitious and explicit goals, we make sure to provide people a platform to implement their talent, and become successful in the competitive market of the 21st Century. For us, the economy of Lithuania, which is growing at a neck-break speed is our home and our inspiration is derived from our belief in change. Our team works together, with a clear and cut image of where we want to be in the future.

Meet The Founder

In 2014, the founder of international solutions platform, Pasha Naserabadi saw the need for connection of people who have a certain set of skills in their niche and came up with Šeštoji Planeta. He is a well-known IT expert of the Middle East IT industry and an ambitious entrepreneur who is studying International Business at Vilnius University, Lithuania.

Our Mission

With the founder leading the company from the front since 2014, the team at Šeštoji Planeta did not take long to start working on exceptional business ideas, while striving to cater each niche differently.

Our aim is to be led by the inspiration, which moved our lead thinker to create this platform. Our vision is to fulfil the needs of the problems, which have been recognized till yet and with this mission statement, we are endeavoring to build a strong community, with the aims of connecting abilities, skills, talents and enhance human interaction.
